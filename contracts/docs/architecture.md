# Medimint Architecture

## 1. Overview

Medimint is designed to bridge real-world healthcare infrastructure with blockchain-based financial systems. The MDMT token acts as the core utility layer connecting diagnostic services, revenue generation, and value distribution within the ecosystem.

---

## 2. How the Token Connects to Healthcare Infrastructure

Medimint integrates directly with diagnostic centers and medical equipment such as MRI, CT, and other imaging modalities.

### Key Flow:
- Patients access diagnostic services through Medimint’s partner network.
- Payments are made via fiat, insurance, or MDMT tokens.
- MDMT can be used for:
  - Discounted healthcare services
  - Priority access
  - Ecosystem participation

### Infrastructure Layer:
- Diagnostic machines are deployed and managed under Medimint’s network.
- These assets generate real-world revenue.
- The blockchain layer records value flow and enables token-based participation.

---

## 3. Revenue → Token Flow

Medimint creates a direct linkage between healthcare revenue and token value.

### Revenue Sources:
- Diagnostic services (MRI, CT, Ultrasound, etc.)
- Hospital partnerships
- Healthcare service integrations

### Token Flow Mechanism:
1. Revenue is generated from real-world healthcare operations.
2. A portion of net profits is allocated to the token ecosystem.
3. Funds are distributed through:
   - Staking rewards
   - Token buybacks
   - Ecosystem reinvestment

### Economic Impact:
- Creates intrinsic value backing for the token.
- Aligns token holder incentives with business performance.
- Enables sustainable long-term growth.

---

## 4. Future Staking & Rewards Logic

Staking will be introduced as a core mechanism for user participation and rewards.

### Staking Model:
- Users lock MDMT tokens in staking contracts.
- Rewards are distributed periodically based on ecosystem revenue.

### Reward Sources:
- Percentage of annual net profits
- Buyback-driven appreciation
- Incentive pools for early adopters

### Expected Features:
- Flexible and fixed staking periods
- Tier-based reward multipliers
- Auto-compounding options

### Governance Layer (Future):
- Token holders will participate in decision-making:
  - Expansion into new geographies
  - Addition of new diagnostic services
  - Allocation of ecosystem funds

---

## 5. Long-Term Vision

Medimint aims to create a scalable healthcare-finance ecosystem where:

- Real-world medical assets generate blockchain-linked value
- Patients benefit from affordable and accessible services
- Token holders participate in revenue and governance

This model transforms healthcare infrastructure into a transparent, participatory, and value-generating digital ecosystem.
